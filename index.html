Course Details

Quran Reading & Tajweed

This comprehensive course is designed for students who want to learn how to read the Quran correctly with proper Tajweed rules. The course covers all the essential aspects of Quranic recitation.

What You'll Learn

  • Correct pronunciation of Arabic letters
  • Basic and advanced Tajweed rules
  • Rules of Noon Sakinah and Tanween
  • Rules of Meem Sakinah
  • Madd (elongation) rules
  • Practical application on Quranic verses

Course Duration

12 weeks (3 months) with 2 sessions per week

Instructor

Ustadha Izba Sutan (CEO & Founder)

Certificate

Free certificate upon successful completion

How Our Classes Work

Our classes are conducted through WhatsApp groups to provide an interactive learning experience. Here's how the process works:

1

Registration

Fill out the registration form with your details and course preference.

2

Verification

3

4

Learning Process

About Us

Our Mission

Rah-e-Noor Quran Site is an online platform created with the vision of bringing women closer to the Quran and authentic Islamic knowledge. In today's busy world, many people — especially women — wish to learn the Quran correctly but find it hard to access reliable and structured guidance. This platform was developed to bridge that gap.


Our Story

Founded by Izba Sutan, Rah e Noor Quran began as a small initiative to teach Quran to a handful of students. Today, we have educated over 5,000 students from around the world through our structured online courses.


What We Offer

  • Carefully structured courses to strengthen understanding
  • Focus on authentic Islamic knowledge from reliable sources
  • Easy Free online registration process
  • Learn at your own pace, from anywhere in the world
  • Short-term courses (2-6 months duration)
  • Free certificates upon course completion
  • Exclusive courses for women and girls

Our Vision

To become a leading platform for authentic Islamic education, empowering Muslims worldwide with knowledge that strengthens their faith and practice.
